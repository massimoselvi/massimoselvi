## 💡 Quick Tips for LinkedIn Icons

* **Don't overdo it:** Use a maximum of 1 emoji per paragraph or 1 per bullet point. Too many colors can make your profile look spammy.
* **Stick to a color theme if possible:** Emojis like ⚡, 🎯, and 🚀 use bright, engaging colors (oranges/yellows/reds) that stand out nicely against LinkedIn’s white/dark background.
* **Whitespace is king:** Always leave a blank line between your paragraphs. Emojis look best when they have room to breathe!
